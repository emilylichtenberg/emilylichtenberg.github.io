Intro

Hi, I'm Emily Lichtenberg and I'm a software engineer.

In college I studied Economics and Transportation Logistics so after graduating I entered a career in Inventory Planning and Operations. I then moved into a strategy-focused role and quickly realized I missed seeing the execution of a project through to the end and wanted to stay involved with the Software Engineers I worked with. From there I decided to switch careers and joined App Academy, an intensive four month coding bootcamp.

When I'm not coding, I like trying new restaurants in NYC, cheering for the Rangers, and watching all reality TV.
